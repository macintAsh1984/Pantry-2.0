What is Order-In-Advance vs Walk-In?

When you order in advance at the pantry, you can go on our website and pick out different produce items such as canned and dry goods. You can reserve a time to pick it up your order at the pantry.
When the pantry is open, we take walk-ins where you can pick up items like fresh produce and personal products in person. A full list of our walk-in item can be found here . Check above for our open hours.


Does the Pantry Accept Donations?

We accept donations in person. You can drop off your food items at the pantry during our open hours. The food items must be unopened and unused. We do not accept homemade goods. What services does the Pantry provide?

  • Walk-In: Simply visit The Pantry (MU 167), swipe/enter UC Davis ID at the front table, and you can enter the space to pick your items!

  • Orders: Visit our website to place an order 24 hours in advance for non-perishable goods! Select a preferred time slot and date to pick up your order at the Pantry.

  • Jar Program (Tuesdays only) Bring in your own containers to get up to two spices!

  • Bag For A Bag (Tuesdays only) Bring in any grocery/plastic/paper bags to get a Pantry tote bag in return! Due to limitations, each UC Davis student is only eligible for one bag.


How much can I take from the Pantry?

Each item has its own limit, so please respect the item limitations written on the signs.


Is everything free?

Yes, everything at the Pantry is free!


Can I visit the Pantry more than once a day?

Yes you can! Please note that our items stay the same throughout the day.
